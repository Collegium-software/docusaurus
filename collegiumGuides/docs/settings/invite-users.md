# Invite Users

As a company owner, you can invite users to join your company. To do so, navigate to **Team Settings**

![Team Settings Menu](/img/teamSettingsMenu.jpg)

Once there, click on **Invite New Users**

![Invite New Users](/img/inviteUsers.png)

Here you can enter the name and email of the person you are trying to add. You should also add the role, this will dictate what the level of access the new user has to your projects.
If you want to add more than one person at a time, click **Add another person** and you can add several more rows which may be populated. When you are happy, click **Submit** and the platform will send emails to all concerned.

![Invite New Users](/img/inviteDialog.png)

Once they follow the onboarding process, they will appear in the **Your Team** table, where you can promote, demote, or remove a user.

![Your Team](/img/yourTeamTable.png)

Once an email has been sent, the new user has 2 days to onboard themselves. From this table, you can click the actions button, and revolk and invite, or if it has expired, reinvite the same person, or delete the invite.

![Invites Table](/img/invitesTable.png)
