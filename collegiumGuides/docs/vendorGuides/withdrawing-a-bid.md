# Withdrawing a Bid

After submitting a bid, conditions may change and you may no longer want to compete for the work package. If the work package has not closed, you can easily withdraw your bid.

## The Work Package Overview Tab

After clicking **View** on the relevant work package, you are taken to the overview tab. Here you can see **Edit Existing Bid** and **Withdraw Bid**.

![Work Package Overview](/img/bidWizzard5.png)

Click on **Withdraw Bid**.

If you still want to withdraw, click **Confirm**.

![Confirm the withdrawal](/img/withdrawBid1.png)

You can now submit a replacement bid if you want to.

![After withdrawing a bid](/img/withdrawBid2.png)

If you navigate to **Your Withdrawn Bids**, you will see your bid listed there.

![Withdrawn Bids](/img/withdrawBid3.png)

By clicking the three dot actions button, you can **Delete** the bid, permanently deleting it, or **Restore** the bid. You can only restore a withdrawn bid if you have not submitted a replacement bid and the work package has not closed. Once restored, the bid becomes available to the work package owner again.

![Withdrawn bids actions](/img/withdrawBid4.png)
